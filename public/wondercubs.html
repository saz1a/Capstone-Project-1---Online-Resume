CFG Group Project: Wonder Cubs!

Wonder Cubs is a website dedicated to empowering young girls through the beautiful game of football! Showcases the incredible talent and achievements of the England Women's football team, affectionately known as 'The Lionesses.', the goal is to provide a fun and educational platform where girls can learn, explore, and actively participate in football.

As My primary responsibility was to design and develop both the 'Home page' and the 'News & Activities page,' ensuring an engaging and informative experience for our young audience, I aimed to create the homepage to be a vibrant hub, filled with captivating articles, inspiring videos, and success story links, all cantered around girl's and women's football.

The 'News & Activities' page was created to be a valuable resource for Wonder Cubs. It provides essential information about the numerous benefits of playing football, encouraging girls to embrace the sport wholeheartedly. It also highlight heartwarming success stories of young girls who have joined football teams, showcasing the positive impact football can have on their lives.
